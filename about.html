Who we are?

We are Bahrain Polytechnic web media students working on a project to develop a website for a business which supports the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Gender equality is the aim we have chosen. We chose this goal due to increases in gendered fights and miscommunications in the last few years.

Our goal is to produce an interesting and entertaining cards game that delivers this message. The game will show how males and females think,

focusing on their equality and the need of understanding and collaboration. It highlights how, even with differences between them, genders depend on each other.
